Team Members

This page introduces the core team members and their roles in the UK–Brazil collaboration. Profile images are shown on the right and can be uploaded later.

Qiuji Yi

Assistant Professor (UK Principal Investigator) · Northumbria University

Dr Qiuji Yi is an Assistant Professor at Northumbria University, United Kingdom. His work focuses on sensing and non-destructive evaluation for engineering systems, combining advanced signal processing with data-driven modelling and experimental validation. Within this project, he leads the UK team and oversees the overall research direction, methodology development, and coordination with the Brazil partner.

Non-Destructive Testing Sensing & Data Processing Eddy-Current / Thermography Project Coordination

Cesar

Professor (Brazil Principal Investigator)

Prof. Cesar is the Brazil Principal Investigator and leads the experimental activities on the Brazil side. His research profile is strongly practice-oriented, with extensive experience in laboratory implementation, instrumentation, and the delivery of experimentally validated engineering solutions. In this collaboration, he contributes to experimental design, platform setup, and cross-validation between modelling and measured data.

Experimental Development Instrumentation Thermography / NDT Validation

Jingyuan Yang

Research Associate · Northumbria University

Dr Jingyuan Yang is a Research Associate at Northumbria University. He received a PhD in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Birmingham, with research centred on electromagnetic non-destructive testing for infrastructure and engineering components. His current work emphasises multi-physics modelling and experimental implementation, including COMSOL-based simulation, MATLAB data analysis, and the development of practical inspection workflows. In this project, he supports numerical modelling, experimental execution, and data interpretation, bridging simulation and measurements across both partners.

COMSOL Modelling MATLAB Data Analysis Eddy Current Thermography Experiment Implementation
