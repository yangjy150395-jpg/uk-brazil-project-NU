Project Summary

Thermographic reconstruction and inversion for wind turbine blades

Project Introduction

Funding, partners, and scope.

This project is funded by the International Science Partnerships Fund (ISPF) under the International Collaboration Awards 2024 – Brazil and South Africa scheme. The awarded grant reference is ICAO/R1/241110, with a total funding value of £208,069.04.

The project is led by Dr Qiuji Yi (Northumbria University, UK) in collaboration with Professor Cesar Giron Camerini (Federal University of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil). It establishes a UK–Brazil research partnership focused on advancing non-destructive testing (NDT) techniques for wind turbine blade inspection, with validation in Brazilian wind-energy contexts.
